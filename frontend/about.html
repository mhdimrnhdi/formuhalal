About This Project

FormuHalal is a digital B2B decision-support system designed specifically for Malaysian SME food manufacturers. When raw materials face shortages, price spikes, or compliance issues, businesses need to quickly find Halal-compliant substitutes without compromising product quality.

Our platform bridges the gap between food science expertise and local supply chain logistics by providing:

  • Chemical property matching for ingredient substitution
  • Verified Malaysian Halal supplier database
  • Real-time compliance checking against JAKIM standards
  • Enterprise-grade dashboard for formulation management

Built with modern web technologies and designed for seamless integration with existing manufacturing workflows.

The Problem

SMEs often lack in-house food science expertise to find Halal-compliant substitutes. Production halts, quality issues, and revenue loss result from ingredient shortages.

Our Solution

An intelligent substitution engine that matches chemical properties with verified local suppliers, enabling rapid pivot decisions while maintaining Halal certification integrity.
